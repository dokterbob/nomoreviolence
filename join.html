Join us

These are some of the ways in which you can get involved:

  • Download, print and spread the poster.
  • Share the message with your friends using Twitter or Facebook.
  • Join one of our local initiatives in Amsterdam, Rotterdam, London or Paris.
  • If there is no local initiative near you, start one!

    Get some friends together, make a plan, go out into the streets and: spread the message. Make sure you document your efforts and post them back to us. We'd love your photographs, tweets and your videos!

Contact us at info@nomoreviolence.tv or at press@nomoreviolence.tv if you're a journalist.
