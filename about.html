A group of people has come together to agree on one thing: the violence which Libian dictator Khadaffi is exorting on his own people is unacceptable. However, the strong economic ties between Libya and other countries distract the attention of some of our leaders away from these humanitarian issues.

This is where we come to take a stand: the Libian people should have the same freedoms we have come to enjoy. They deserve their freedom and safety. Right now, by any means necessary. Regardless of economical consequences: human lives are invaluable.

Concretely, we expect our leaders to urge the United Nations to take swift action to protect the Libyan people. This does not imply the occupation of another Arab country, it might come in the form of a short-term stabilization army. It might also come in the form of a no-fly zone over the Western parts of Libya.

We will not stand for another genocide while the world watches with hollow eyes. Take action. Now.
